Competition Strategy

Team Focus

For this competition season, our team decided to design a new carbon fiber hull. This would give new team members the opportunities to help design the boat as well as work with new materials like carbon fiber. Experienced members would guide the newer members but would still incorporate new and fresh ideas allowing for greater in-person/team bonding opportunities that were absent from the last few years due to covid.

Thrust-to-Weight Ratio

The usage of carbon fiber hulls instead of foam hulls means that the boat is much lighter. To account for the reduced weight, less thrusters are needed, which is why we decided on using a four-thruster setup.
